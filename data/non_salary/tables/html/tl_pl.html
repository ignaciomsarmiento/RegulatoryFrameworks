  • At least 20 years: 35 calendar days.

ECUADOR
Paid leave
At least 1 year: 15 calendar days.

After 5 years, workers have the right to one additional vacation day per additional year worked, up to a maximum of 15 extra days, or the equivalent monetary compensation.

Workers younger than 16: 20 calendar days. Workers between 16 and 18: 18 calendar days.

MEXICO
Paid leave
Varying by length of tenure:
  • 1 year: 12 working days.
  • After the first year, vacations increase by 2 days per year. Starting in the fifth year, vacations increase by 2 days per every 5 years until they reach a maximum of 32 days.
