Contribution rate
Salaried employees: 9%.

Self-employed workers:
Autonomos: 0%.
Monotributo General: Fixed amount in ARS varying by sector of activity, annual revenues, size and cost of physical space, and electric energy consumption. Between ARS 19,239.97 (0.06 MW) and ARS 65,806.30 (0.20 MW) in June 2025.
Salaried employees contribution rate, by statutory payer:
Employee: 3%.
Employer: 6%.
Contributory base (if different than contributory base for pensions)

Additional contribution for dependent beneficiaries coverage
Dependents are covered.
Additional contributions are required only for monotributistas:same as worker contribution per insured additional member.

MEXICO
Contribution rate
Salaried employees:
- Workers with a Wage Contribution Base (WCB) equal to or less than 3 Federal MW: 27.8% over 1 MW.
- Workers with WCB greater than 3 Federal MW: 27.8% over 1 MW plus 8% over (WCB - 3 Federal MW).
- Additional 1% contribution for in-cash services (paid sick days and disability pensions) over WCB.

Self-employed workers: 0%.
Salaried employees contribution rate, by statutory payer:
Employee:
- Workers with WCB equal to or less than 3 MW active in the Federal District: 0%.
- Workers with WCB greater than 3 MW active in the Federal District:
2% over (WCB-3 Federal MW).
- All workers: 0.25% over WBC for in-cash services.

Employer:
- Workers with WCB equal to or less than 3 MW active in the Federal District: 13.9% over 1 Federal MW.
- Workers with WCB greater than 3 MW active in the Federal District: 13.9% over 1 Federal MW + 6% over (WCB - 3 Federal MW).
- Additional 0.6% contribution over WCB for in-cash services.

Government: 13.9% over 1 Federal MW + 0.05% for in-cash services.
Contributory base (if different than contributory base for pensions)
Salaried employees:
- For basic 13.9% contribution: 1 Federal MW.
- For additional contribution: (WCB - 3 Federal MW).
- For in-cash services contribution: WCB.

Minimum contribution base: 1 Federal MW.
Maximum contribution base: 25 Federal MW.
Additional contribution for dependent beneficiaries coverage
Dependents are covered.
Legislation
Social Security Law (1995, last reformed in 2023).
General Health Law (1984, last reformed in 2023).
