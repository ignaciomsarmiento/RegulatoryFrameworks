BOLIVIA
Annual and other periodic bonuses
Christmas bonus: 1 monthly wage.

Employment tenure bonus "Antigüedad": 3 MW multiplied by a factor that varies with length of tenure:
  • 2-4 years: 5%.
  • 5-7 years: 11%.
  • 8-10 years: 18%.
  • 11-14 years: 26%.
  • 15-19 years: 34%.
  • 20-24 years: 42%.
  • 25 or more years: 50%.
Quinquennial bonus: Employees who have been in service for at least 5 years are entitled to five monthly wages paid every five years.

Economic growth bonus "Doble aguinaldo": 1 monthly wage awarded when the annual June-to-June GDP growth exceeds 4.5%.

Only employees working within 50km of international borders: Border subsidy: 20% of wages.
Legislation
Law of December 18th (1944) - Christmas bonus.
Supreme Decree 21137 (1985) - Bono antiguedad and border subsidy.
Supreme Decree 522 (2010) - Bono quinquenio.
Supreme Decree 1802 (2013) - Doble aguinaldo.

PERU
Annual and other periodic bonuses
Annual bonus: Workers in medium and large businesses: 2 wages + corresponding employer health contribution (9% or 6.75%, depending on modality), annual.

Family bonus: a monthly bonus equivalent to 10% of the MW for workers who have children under 18 or children under 24 attending tertiary education. This bonus is per worker, not per child.

Workers in small and micro businesses: 1 wage + corresponding employer health contribution (9% or 6.75%, depending on modality), annual.
Legislation
Law No. 25129 (1989) - Family Bonus.
Legislative Decree No. 713 (1991) - Annual leave.
Law No. 27735 (2002) - Annual Bonus.
Supreme Decree 013-2013-PRODUCE (2013) - Bonus and annual leave for micro and small businesses.
