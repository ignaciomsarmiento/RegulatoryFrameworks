ARGENTINA
Contribution rate
Salaried employees: Insurance premium is made up of a fixed sum per insured employee and a variable rate over the payroll, negotiated between insurance companies and employers. In June 2023, the fixed component was valued at ARS 262 (0.003 MW). At an aggregate level, variable premiums were equivalent to 2.53% of payroll in June 2023.

Self-employed workers:
Autonomos in risky activities: 3%.
Monotributo General: voluntary individual insurance.
Salaried employees contribution rate, by statutory payer:
Paid by employer.
Contributory base (if different than contributory base for pensions)

Law 24557 (1995).
Decree 590 (1997).
Labor Risks Superintendence Disposition 6 (2023).

Contribution rate

Base premium: 0.93%.
Variable premium: 0-6.8%, depending on the business-specific accident rate.

Self-employed workers earning less than 5MW per year: Voluntary contributions are allowed at the same rates as for salaried employees.
